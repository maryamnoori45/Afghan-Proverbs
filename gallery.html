صبر تلخ است ولی میوه‌اش شیرین

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

This proverb teaches the value of patience and how difficult situations lead to rewards over time.

آب که از سر گذشت، چه یک وجب چه صد وجب

Once the water passes the head, it doesn't matter if it's an inch or a hundred.

This proverb means that once a situation has gone beyond control, there's no point in worrying about its magnitude.

هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

The higher the roof, the more snow it catches.

Those with more power or wealth face greater responsibilities and challenges in life.

دیوار موش دارد، موش گوش دارد

The wall has mice, and the mice have ears.

This proverb teaches the idea that secrets or plans can often be overheard or leaked, no matter how secure a situation appears.

.نابرده رنج،گنج میسر نمی شود

No pain, no gain.

Used to emphasize that success requires effort and hard work.

. از دل برود هر آنکه از دیده برفت

Out of sight, out of mind.

Used when someone or something is forgotten because they are no longer present.
